Pumpkin Turkey Chili

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped yellow bell pepper
  • 1 clove garlic,minced
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups pumkin puree
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons chili powder or more to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 dash salt
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

    • Step 1

    Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion,green bell pepper,yellow bell pepper,and garlic; cook and stir until tender

    Step 2

    Stir in turkey and cook until evenly brown; drain

    Step 3

    Mix in tomatoes and pumkin. Season witch chili powder,pepper,and salt. Reduce heat to low,cover,and simmer 15 to 20 minutes. Serve topped with Cheddar cheese and sour cream.
