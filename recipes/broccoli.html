Broccoli Chicken Divan

Chiken Divan with broccoli is a quick and easy casserole that's sure to be a hit with the family!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chopped fresh broccoli
  • 1 1/2 cups cubed,cooked chiken meat
  • 1 (10.5 ounce)can condesed cream of broccoli soup
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons dried bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon butter,melted

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F(230 degrees C).

Step 2

Place broccoli in saucepan with enough water to cover. Bring to a boil and cook until tender,about 5 minutes.Drain.

Step 3

Place the cooked broccoli in a 9 inch pie plate. Top with the chiken. In a bowl,mix the soup and milk,and pour over the chiken. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese. Mix the melted butter with the bread crumbs,and sprinkle over the cheese

Step 4

Baje in the preheated oven until casserole is bubbly and the top is golden brown,about 15 minutes.
